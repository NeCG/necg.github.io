The Computer Graphics Student Group of FEUP, NeCG|FEUP, was created back in 2006 by a group of undergraduate Informatics and Computing Engineering students who were passionate about Computer Graphics. After a few years of inactivity, we (the newcomers of 2013) are now aiming efforts to revive the group in a new format featuring three related areas: Computer Graphics, Interaction and Game Development.

People from all kinds of backgrounds are welcome here: from the simple enthusiast to the computer graphics programmer, from the design specialist to the game experience analyst. We want to give people a chance to work here on what they really like to do.

Without our teaching mentors none of this would be possible, and since our creation we have been strongly supported by the Department of Informatics Engineering (DEI) of the Engineering Faculty of Porto University (FEUP).
